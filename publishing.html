Pearson Reading Program Teacher’s Edition prototype—Grade 5

While working at rosa+wesley, inc. I worked on design prototyping for Pearson Scott Foresman. I worked closely with the client’s design director and editorial leads.

In this Grade 5 English Teacher’s edition and the Spanish language adaptation, I worked to add additional layers of instructional information in a simple, well organized manner that will help busy teachers work effectively with students of all learning styles and abilities.
